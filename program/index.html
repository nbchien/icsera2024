Transportation & Venue

The venue for ICSERA 2024 is the main campus of Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology and Education (HCMUTE) (www.hcmute.edu.vn). Located at the eastern gate of Ho Chi Minh City, the campus is just 10 km northeast of the city center and 17 km from Tan Son Nhat International Airport.

By Taxi

In Ho Chi Minh City, taxis are readily available, making it easy to hail a ride at any time. Among the many taxi brands operating in the city, we recommend Mai Linh and Vinasun for their reliability, convenience, and reasonable fares.

Ride-Hailing (Grab)

Alternatively, you can use Grab, a popular ride-hailing service (Uber is not available in Vietnam). Grab offers competitive rates, promotions, and real-time tracking. You will have access to the driver's details and exact fare upfront. Note: A 4G or Wi-Fi connection is required.

Destination Address for Driver/Grab:

- No.1, Vo Van Ngan Street, Linh Chieu Ward, Thu Duc City, Ho Chi Minh City

- Số 1, Võ Văn Ngân, Phường Linh Chiểu, Thành phố Thủ Đức, Thành phố Hồ Chí Minh
