ICEGJ History

ICEGJ aims to bridge the gap between green skills education and labor market demands by uniting higher education institutions, industry leaders, and policymakers across the ASEAN and EU regions.

Conference Venue

The 1st International Conference and Exhibition of Green Jobs is organized at Hanoi University of Science and Technology (HUST), Vietnam.

Conference Proceedings

The Conference Proceedings containing the extended abstracts presented will be distributed in USB. The USB will include papers accepted for presentation, provided at least one author has pre-registered by the deadline.
